Towards Open Policy Agent 1.0 December 28th marked the 8th anniversary of the first commit in the Open Policy Agent project. 5000+ commits from more than 400 contributors later, we’re starting to prepare for OPA 1.0. Following the rules of semantic versioning, one would be excused to think of 1.0 as the first “stable” version.
