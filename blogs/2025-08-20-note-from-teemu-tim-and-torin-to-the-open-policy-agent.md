Today we’re excited to announce that the creators of Open Policy Agent (along with many team members from Styra) have joined Apple to continue our decade-long mission of delivering an open source solution to unifying policy across the cloud-native stack. Apple is an enthusiastic OPA user, utilizing it as a key component of its authorization infrastructure to manage a vast portfolio of global-scale cloud services. Today’s announcement demonstrates Apple’s commitment to the OPA project by making a larger investment in the technology and the community.
